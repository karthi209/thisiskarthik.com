Meta

This site is just HTML and CSS compiled into static pages. I wrote a blog generator in Go because I got tired of platforms that change their terms of service every Tuesday and frameworks whose dependencies break every Friday.

A full build takes {{.BuildTime}} milliseconds. That's longer than zero, but shorter than most modern websites take to load. So there's that.

Almost everything lives in the static build with few moving parts means fewer things to break in ways I'll discover at 2am.

Typography

I spent way too much time picking fonts and here's what I ended up with:

Headings use IM Fell Great Primer. It's a revival of some eighteenth-century typeface. I picked it because it looks serious without trying too hard, which is more than I can say for most things on the internet.

Body text uses Libertinus Serif. It's plenty readable, calm, and doesn't feel like it's shouting at you.

Both fonts are open source and served locally, which is nice because I don't trust Google Fonts to still exist in 2030.
